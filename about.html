Our Story

Ontario Demo Pros has been a leader in the demolition industry since 2005, serving clients across Toronto, the GTA, and Southern Ontario. With a passion for precision and safety, we’ve built a reputation for tackling projects of all sizes—from small home renovations to massive industrial teardowns.

  • Over 15 years of demolition expertise.
  • Fully insured and WSIB compliant.
  • Serving residential, commercial, and industrial clients.

Our team is equipped to handle everything from hazardous material removal to structural dismantling, ensuring every job meets the highest standards of safety and efficiency. Contact us at (647) 834-8972 to learn how we can assist with your next project!
